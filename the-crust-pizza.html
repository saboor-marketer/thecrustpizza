Our Story

Built From Passion,
Baked With Purpose

Founded in 2020 by Asad Zeeshan, The Crust Pizza was born with one vision — to create a powerful local pizza identity rooted in Gujranwala. What started as one outlet has grown into a beloved multi-city chain across Punjab and Azad Kashmir.

Day-to-day excellence is driven by GM Shahzaib Tanveer and a passionate team dedicated to fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and experiences that keep customers returning again and again.
